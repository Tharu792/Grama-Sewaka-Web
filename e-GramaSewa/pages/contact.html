Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my application status?

You can track your application status by logging into your account and visiting the "Track Application" section. You'll receive real-time updates on your application progress.

What documents do I need for certificate applications?

Required documents vary by certificate type. Generally, you'll need your National ID, proof of residence, and relevant supporting documents. Check the specific requirements for each service.

How long does it take to process applications?

Processing times vary by service type. Birth certificates typically take 3-5 business days, while other certificates may take 5-10 business days. You'll receive notifications at each stage.

Can I apply for multiple certificates at once?

Yes, you can apply for multiple certificates simultaneously. Each application will be processed independently, and you can track them separately in your dashboard.

What if I need help with the application process?

Our support team is available 24/7 via the hotline (1919) and email. You can also visit our office during working hours for in-person assistance.
