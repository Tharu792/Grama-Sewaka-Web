1. Introduction

Welcome to e-GramaSewa

e-GramaSewa is a comprehensive digital platform that provides easy access to essential government services. Our mission is to simplify and streamline the process for citizens to access government certificates and services online.

Fast & Efficient

Get your certificates and documents processed quickly with our streamlined digital system.

Transparent Process

Track your application status in real-time with complete transparency.

Secure & Safe

Your data is protected with state-of-the-art security measures.

System Requirements

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Internet connection
  • Valid email address
  • National ID or relevant identification documents

2. Getting Started

How to Access e-GramaSewa

1
Visit the Website

Open your web browser and navigate to the e-GramaSewa website.

2
Explore Services

Browse through the available services on the homepage to understand what's offered.

3
Create Account

Register for a new account or login if you already have one.

Tip

You can browse services without registering, but you'll need an account to apply for certificates.

3. User Registration

Creating Your Account

1
Click Register

On the homepage, click the "Register" button in the top-right corner.

2
Fill Registration Form

Complete all required fields in the registration form:

  • Username (must be unique)
  • Password (strong password recommended)
  • Full Name
  • Address
  • NIC Number
  • Telephone Number
  • Email Address
  • Province, District, and Divisional Secretariat
3
Submit Registration

Review your information and click "Register" to create your account.

Important

Make sure all information is accurate as it will be used for certificate applications. You cannot change your username after registration.

4. Login Process

Accessing Your Account

1
Click Login

On the homepage, click the "Login" button.

2
Enter Credentials

Enter your username and password in the login form.

3
Complete reCAPTCHA

Check the "I'm not a robot" box to verify you're human.

4
Access Dashboard

After successful login, you'll be redirected to your role-specific dashboard.

User Roles

Customer

Citizens who apply for government services and certificates.

Grama Sewaka

Government officials who review and process applications.

Admin

System administrators who manage users and system settings.

6. Customer Guide

Customer Dashboard

After logging in as a customer, you'll access your personalized dashboard with the following features:

Applying for Services

1
Select Service

Choose the service you need from the available options on your dashboard.

2
Fill Application Form

Complete all required fields in the application form with accurate information.

3
Upload Documents

Upload all required supporting documents in the specified format (PDF, JPG, PNG).

4
Submit Application

Review your application and click "Submit" to send it for processing.

Tracking Applications

1
Access Track Application

Click on "Track Application" in your dashboard menu.

2
View Status

See the current status of all your applications: Pending, Approved, or Rejected.

3
Check Details

Click on any application to view detailed information and processing history.

Managing Profile

1
Access My Profile

Click on "My Profile" in the navigation menu.

2
Update Information

Modify your personal information as needed.

3
Save Changes

Click "Save" to update your profile information.

7. Grama Sewaka Guide

Grama Sewaka Dashboard

As a Grama Sewaka, you have access to applications from your divisional secretariat area.

Reviewing Applications

1
View Pending Applications

Your dashboard shows all pending applications for your divisional secretariat.

2
Select Application

Click on any application to view detailed information and uploaded documents.

3
Review Documents

Carefully review all submitted documents and information for accuracy.

4
Make Decision

Choose to Approve, Reject, or keep the application Pending based on your review.

Application Status Options

Approve

Application meets all requirements and can be approved.

Reject

Application has issues and must be rejected with a reason.

Pending

Application needs more information or additional review.

Viewing Statistics

Your dashboard provides statistics on:

  • Total applications processed
  • Approved applications count
  • Rejected applications count
  • Pending applications count

8. Admin Guide

Admin Dashboard

System Overview

Viewing All Applications

2
Filter Applications

3
Monitor Processing

Track application processing times and identify bottlenecks.

9. Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions

Login Problems

Can't Login?

  • Check your username and password for typos
  • Ensure Caps Lock is not enabled
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try using a different web browser
  • Contact support if the problem persists

Registration Issues

Registration Failed?

  • Ensure all required fields are filled
  • Check that your username is unique
  • Verify your email address format
  • Make sure your password meets requirements
  • Check your internet connection

File Upload Problems

Can't Upload Documents?

  • Check file size (should be under 5MB)
  • Ensure file format is supported (PDF, JPG, PNG)
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try uploading one file at a time
  • Clear browser cache and try again

Application Status Issues

Application Not Updating?

  • Refresh your browser page
  • Check if you're logged into the correct account
  • Wait a few minutes and check again
  • Contact your Grama Sewaka for status updates

Browser Compatibility

For the best experience, use one of these supported browsers:

  • Google Chrome (recommended)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
