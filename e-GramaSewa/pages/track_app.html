Track Your Application

Monitor the status of your government service applications in real-time

How to Track Your Application

Follow these simple steps to track your government service applications

1

Get Your Application ID

After submitting your application, you will receive a unique Application ID via email. Keep this ID safe as you'll need it for tracking.

2

Enter Your Details

Fill in the tracking form with your Application ID, registered email address, and select the service type you applied for.

3

Click Track Now

Click the "Track Now" button to retrieve your application status and progress information.

4

View Your Status

Review your application status, progress percentage, and expected completion date in the results section.

Important Tips

Check your email regularly for status updates and notifications

Processing times vary by service type (3-10 business days)

Contact support at 1919 if you have any questions

Keep your Application ID confidential for security
