Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on e-GramaSewa?

To create an account, click on the "Register" button in the top navigation, fill in your personal details, verify your email address, and complete the identity verification process using your National ID.

What documents do I need for certificate applications?

Required documents vary by service type. Generally, you'll need your National ID, proof of residence, and relevant supporting documents. Check the specific requirements for each service before applying.

How long does it take to process applications?

Processing times vary by service type. Birth certificates typically take 3-5 business days, while other certificates may take 5-10 business days. You'll receive notifications at each stage of the process.

Can I track my application status?

Yes, you can track your application status by logging into your account and visiting the "Track Application" section. You'll receive real-time updates on your application progress.

What if I forget my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password securely.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use state-of-the-art security measures including SSL encryption, secure servers, and strict data protection protocols to ensure your personal information is safe and secure.

Can I apply for multiple certificates at once?

Yes, you can apply for multiple certificates simultaneously. Each application will be processed independently, and you can track them separately in your dashboard.

What if I need help with the application process?

Our support team is available 24/7 via the hotline (1919) and email. You can also visit our office during working hours for in-person assistance.
